# Facet Meta-Map（地圖的地圖）

## Purpose
Define what qualifies as a Facet before any Facet is listed or designed.

## What a Facet IS
- A structural pressure or state dimension
- Exists independently of specific life events
- Can coexist with other facets
- Can be recalculated and revised
- Can be expressed through multiple metaphysical systems

## What a Facet IS NOT
- A life domain label (e.g. marriage, career)
- A personality trait
- A fixed destiny or outcome
- A narrative-only category

## Evaluation Criteria (All Required)
1. Structural (not event-based)
2. System-agnostic
3. Reversible / recalculable
4. Parallelizable with other facets
5. Longitudinally meaningful

## Advisor Involvement
- Advisors may validate human resonance only
- Advisors must not define structure or system logic

Status: ACTIVE (P0-2)
